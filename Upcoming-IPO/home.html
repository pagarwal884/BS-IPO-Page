How to Subscribe to an IPO?

  • Step 1: Login to your respective service provider.
  • Step 2: Click on the IPO button
  • Step 3: Select the IPO and enter the details
  • Step 4: Make payment or authorize mandate

Should I buy an IPO first day?

  • If oversubscribed, IPOs often list at a premium.
  • IPOs from known, profitable brands perform well.
  • Always read the DRHP and understand the risks.
